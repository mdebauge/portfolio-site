Link Light Rail

The Link is Seattle's public light rail service which runs from Sea-Tac airport through Downtown Seattle and recently has grown to include stops to the Capitol Hill neighborhood and the University of Washington. Starting with a creative brief, the Brand Positioning, Brand Character, and Company Promise was determined in order to guide the branding process. Deliverables included an identity manual (with logo, colors, voice & tone), trains wrap, print assets (business cards, tickets, brochures, ads, etc.), web assets (website, mobile app), as well as wayfinding system, icons, and signage.

Brand Positioning

For commuters, Link Light Rail is the transit solution that provides an affordable, pleasant experience. Our brand is chosen because it is the only reliable transit option that operates outside the flow of traffic and improves the flow of your day.

Brand Character

We are dependable, approachable, and forward thinking.

Company Promise

We believe as Seattle grows we need a reliable solution to keep the city moving. That's why we bring you accessible transit for today and continually improve to meet the needs of tomorrow.
