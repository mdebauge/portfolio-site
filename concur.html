Concur Labs

The Concur Labs team goal is to surface innovations and look at problems differently, often combining technologies in novel ways to enhance the life of a business traveler. As part of the team, I collaborated with engineers, product managers, and designers to test new concepts, explore ideas and build prototypes intended to innovate and advance the Concur Platform.

My role included collaborating on the user experience of the projects, creating UI for the web and mobile prototypes, and building microsites to showcase the projects.

Personal Cards

It’s time-consuming to track, identify and log business-related charges from a personal credit card—and then create a cash transaction—just to be reimbursed. This expense service simplifies the process: Login to your personal bank account. Click a button to identify business expenses. Instantly have your expenses appear in Concur.
I was in charge of the user experience and front-end development of the web app.

Share It

Booking travel with groups is hard to coordinate. First, you have to plan flight schedules and see what’s available at the same time. Then you have to find and book hotels in the right location for meetings. And then you have to contact everyone in your group to make sure they have the exact same travel details. With Share It, you share your Concur itinerary in one text message, and then book same or similar flights from there.
I collaborated on the user experience and UI design of the mobile app. I also designed and built the microsite showcasing this project.

LeaveTime

Getting to the airport can sometimes be a hassle...especially during peak times. LeaveTime focuses on notifying users when to leave for the airport based on the current traffic and airport security lines.
I worked on the user experience and designed the UI and screens for the app idea.

Attend It

You had a productive meeting over dinner in France. Now you’re home and can’t recall how many co-workers attended and how many clients made it out to the meeting. Expense chaos. With the “AttendIt” concept, you simply view and confirm attendees right from your iOS calendar with one tap. Instead of manually entering your report or receipts and trying to remember who came, you can quickly identify participants (or “attendees”) from your calendar event in one tap from your device. Accurate data goes to your Concur account, which double-checks for duplications, and there you have easy, instantaneous reporting.
I collaborated on the user experience and UI design of the mobile app. I also designed and built the microsite showcasing this project.
