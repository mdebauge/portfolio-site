Growth App

The Growth app's goal is to spark interest in the stock market in young students and provide an outlet for parents and teachers to connect, reward, and help educate students on the importance of saving and investment. The app provides a playful, interactive interface and offers queues that encourage exploration, education, and investment.

Kids receive virtual money within the app and required to buy stock with it. They then get the excitement of watching their investment grow or shrink over an accelerated time frame that is tied to the actual market.
